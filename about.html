About Voting System

The Voting System is a decentralized application (DApp) that facilitates secure and transparent voting processes using blockchain technology.

Features:

  • Secure and tamper-proof voting
  • Transparent candidate selection and voting status
  • Allowlist management for authorized voters
  • Efficient and fair election results calculation

How to Connect and Install MetaMask for Voting:

  1. Visit MetaMask website to download and install MetaMask extension for your browser (available for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge).
  2. Open MetaMask and set up your wallet by creating a new account or importing an existing one.
  3. Connect your MetaMask wallet to the Ethereum mainnet or a test network like Ropsten or Rinkeby.
  4. Once connected, navigate to the Voting System DApp and click on the "Connect Wallet" button.
  5. Authorize the connection between MetaMask and the Voting System DApp.
  6. Your wallet is now connected, and you can participate in secure and transparent voting!
